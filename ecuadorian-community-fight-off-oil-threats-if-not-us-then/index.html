If Not Us Then Who?

Voices of Amerikua featured in “If Not Us Then Who”

Ecuadorian community fight off oil threats - If Not Us Then Who?

The community of Morete Cocha is located in the region of Pastaza Province in the Amazon region of Ecuador. Like many other communities Morete Cocha is under threat by interest of oil companies seeking to exploit crude in the communities land. This is the story of community's search for environmental and cultural preservation.
